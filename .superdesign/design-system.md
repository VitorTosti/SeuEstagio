# SeuEstagio Design System

## Brand

- Product: SeuEstagio
- Positioning: A focused internship discovery platform that helps university students find relevant opportunities and take the next step into their careers.
- Personality: optimistic, practical, trustworthy, energetic, and student-first.
- Voice: concise Brazilian Portuguese, encouraging without sounding childish or overly corporate.

## Visual Direction

- Direction: Crisp SaaS Launch.
- The interface should feel like a real, polished product rather than a generic marketing template.
- Use generous white space, clear hierarchy, restrained depth, and a prominent product-search experience in the first viewport.
- Avoid purple, neon colors, excessive gradients, oversized decorative shapes, and overly rounded cards.

## Color

- Ink: #102A2A
- Deep teal: #0D5C5A
- Primary green: #1B8F5A
- Bright green accent: #42C47A
- Soft mint surface: #EAF7F0
- Pale cyan surface: #EAF6F7
- Warm highlight: #F5C451
- White: #FFFFFF
- Border: #D8E5E2
- Muted text: #5E7371
- Use the primary green for high-intent actions and the deep teal for navigation and strong headings.
- Warm yellow may appear sparingly for saved jobs, ratings, or small highlights.

## Typography

- Use a modern sans serif with excellent Portuguese readability, such as Inter or Manrope.
- Hero heading: 52-60px desktop, 36-42px mobile, weight 700, compact line-height, letter-spacing 0.
- Section headings: 32-40px desktop, 28-32px mobile.
- Body: 16-18px, relaxed line-height.
- UI labels: 13-15px, medium weight.

## Layout

- Desktop max content width: 1180-1240px.
- Header: compact, white, logo left, navigation centered/right, login secondary, primary CTA.
- Hero: two-column composition with copy and search actions on the left and a realistic job discovery product preview on the right.
- Keep a visible hint of the next section below the fold.
- Use full-width page bands with constrained inner content; do not wrap entire sections in floating cards.
- Cards are reserved for individual job listings, testimonials, and small product modules; maximum radius 8px.

## Page Structure

1. Compact navigation with SeuEstagio wordmark and CTA.
2. Hero with literal value proposition, location/course search, and product UI preview.
3. Trust strip with universities or employer credibility signals.
4. Three-step flow: discover, compare, apply.
5. Featured internship listings with salary, location, modality, company, and saved-state controls.
6. Benefits split for students and partner companies.
7. Student success proof with one concise testimonial and useful metrics.
8. FAQ.
9. Strong closing CTA and compact footer.

## Components

- Primary buttons: green fill, white label, 44-48px height, 6-8px radius.
- Secondary buttons: white or transparent with subtle border.
- Search fields: labeled inputs with familiar search/location icons and a clear submit button.
- Job cards: scan-friendly rows or compact cards, not decorative tiles. Include company logo, role, company, tags, pay, location, modality, and save icon.
- Tags: compact status chips only where useful; do not use pills as decoration.
- Icons: Lucide-style line icons, consistent 18-20px size.
- Product preview: believable internship search UI with filters, results, and one selected role detail.

## Content Direction

- Primary headline: "Seu estágio começa aqui."
- Supporting copy should explain that students can discover opportunities aligned with their course, location, and goals.
- Primary CTA: "Encontrar vagas"
- Secondary CTA: "Para empresas"
- Prefer concrete labels such as "Engenharia de Software", "São Paulo", "Híbrido", "R$ 1.800/mês", and "Candidatura rápida".
- Avoid vague startup jargon and exaggerated claims.

## Responsive Behavior

- Mobile hero stacks copy, search, then product preview.
- Search fields become a vertical group with a full-width submit button.
- Job cards preserve role, company, location, pay, and apply/save actions without truncation.
- Navigation collapses to a familiar menu icon.
- No text or controls may overlap at narrow widths.
